Visit Schweinfurt

Schweinfurt has been world-famous as an industrial city for more than 100 years. But Schweinfurt is more then that.

Top three activities to do at Schweinfurt

Georg Schäfer Museum

It houses a unique collection of German paintings and drawings from 1760 to 1930.

Stadtstrand

The Schweinfurt city beach invites you to take a short vacation. Enjoy the sun, sand, water, good cocktails, snacks and a chilled atmosphere.

Rathaus

Schweinfurt Town Hall is the city's most important and significant building from the Renaissance era.
